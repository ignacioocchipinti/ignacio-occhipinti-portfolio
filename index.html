About me

My name is Ignacio, but everyone calls me Nacho. I was born on December 11, 1998, so if my math is correct I'm 26 years old.

Passionate about the audiovisual world, I pursued a career in Multimedia Arts at the National University of Arts. In 2023, I got my Associate Degree and now I'm preparing to get my Bachelor Degree.

Why get in contact with me? I am passionate about learning and seek to absorb everything in the design/development world, challenging myself to achieve my best version and expand my knowledge. I ensure a beneficial experience for both me and you, performing to the best of my ability and helping where I can.

PALAN'T

Video game with motion capture based on light tracking and physics libraries developed in Processing. The game's narrative is based on the figure of a politician whose goal is to collect money while avoiding shovels that metaphorically represent the act of working. Additionally, the politician will be hindered by bills that slow down their movements. It is an endless game; as certain amounts of money are collected, the difficulty increases, and the player who surpasses the most difficulty levels is the winner.

Processing

Nowacki voice creator

This is a program developed in p5.js that, based on audio capture and its analysis in terms of frequency and amplitude, replicates works created by the artist Andrzej Nowacki. At the beginning, you choose which work of the artist you want to replicate. Then, based on the amplitude, each part is drawn, and based on the frequency used, colors are selected. Finally, your final work can be downloaded.
